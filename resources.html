Interview Preparation

Common Interview Questions:

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Why do you want this job/internship?
  • Describe a time you solved a problem.
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Interview Tips:

  • Research the company before the interview
  • Practice answering questions out loud
  • Dress professionally (business casual or formal)
  • Arrive early or log in 5-10 minutes before
  • Use clear and confident communication

Mock Interview Checklist:

  • Resume printed and ready
  • Notebook and pen for notes
  • Prepared questions to ask interviewer
  • Strong handshake / eye contact (or virtual presence)

Key Skills to Demonstrate:

  • Communication skills
  • Problem-solving ability
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Confidence under pressure
  • Knowledge of the company and role

Common Mistakes to Avoid:

  • Not researching the company
  • Speaking too fast or too quietly
  • Giving vague answers
  • Not asking questions at the end
